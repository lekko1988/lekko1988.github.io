    Cross innovation Lab(CiL)Permalink

    The CiL Lab focuses on the intersection of emotion-centered research, particularly in the areas of psychology and computer science.

    We are specifically focusing on the following areas:

    • Research on theoretical mechanism construction and techniques for quantifying emotions;
    • Vision-based large-scale computational psychiatry applications;
    • Brain imaging and computational psychiatry technology research;
    • Research on multimodal techniques;
    • Research on human-centered privacy-preserving technologies;
    • etc.

    We are based in the School of Psychology, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
