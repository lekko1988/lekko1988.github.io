    I am a Research Assistant Professor at the School of Psychology, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I lead the Cross innovation Lab(CiL). I am an Area Editor of CAAI Artificial Intelligence Research, and also as a senior member of China Computer Federation(CCF), a member of the Chinese Psychological Society and a member of Affective Computing Specialised Committee, Chinese Information Society of China. My research interests include Computational Affection, Quantitative Affective Computing and Computational Psychiatry.

    Previously I was as an Visitor Scholar at MSIIP Lab in Tsinghua University(Oct.2021-Dec.2021) and New York University Shanghai(Aug.2023-Dec.2024).

    We have multiple openings for Visitor Student/Internship positions. Please make contact for further information by email.
